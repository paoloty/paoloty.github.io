Journey to Discover the Greatness of the Filipino Heart

What is the Filipino Story??

It is a six-part animated series that tells the unifying story of the Filipino people in an entertaining and inspiring way.

The Animated Series

We will take you on an adventure through the history of the Filipino people, experience what it is that makes us beautiful, and don’t be surprised if you feel something stir up in your heart along the way.

The Creator's Story

Tony Olaes, a Fil-Am, didn’t even want to be Filipino growing up in the US. But a transformative visit to the Philippines not only changed the way he saw Filipinos, but awakened his true identity.

Join us on this journey together to understand more about who you are and where you come from. Be the first to get exciting new content every two weeks. Help us reach our goal of 1 million people on the boat.
